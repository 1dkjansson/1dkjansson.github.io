Pumpkin Bread

Moist, spiced, and perfect for fall.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ¾ cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp nutmeg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup pumpkin purée
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, mix pumpkin, sugar, eggs, oil, and water.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Pour into pan.
  5. Bake for 50–60 minutes. Let cool before slicing.
