Pumpkin Bread

← Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ginger
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin
  • 1/3 cup water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan.
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, salt, and spices.
  3. In another bowl, beat sugar, oil, eggs, pumpkin, and water.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Pour into pan and bake 60–70 minutes.
  6. Let cool before slicing.
