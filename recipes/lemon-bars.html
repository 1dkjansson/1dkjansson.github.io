Lemon Bars

← Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix 1 cup flour, powdered sugar, and butter. Press into a greased 8-inch square pan.
  3. Bake crust for 15–20 minutes or until lightly golden.
  4. In a bowl, whisk eggs, sugar, 2 tbsp flour, baking powder, and lemon juice.
  5. Pour over hot crust. Bake for another 20–25 minutes.
  6. Cool completely and dust with powdered sugar before cutting.
