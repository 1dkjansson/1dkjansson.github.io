Carrot Cake

← Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp nutmeg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • ½ cup crushed pineapple (drained)
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients. In another bowl, beat sugar, oil, and eggs.
  3. Stir in carrots, pineapple, and nuts. Add dry mix.
  4. Pour into pan and bake 35–40 minutes. Cool and frost.
