Blueberry Muffins

← Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a muffin tin with liners.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  3. In another bowl, whisk oil, egg, and milk.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Fold in blueberries.
  5. Fill muffin cups about 2/3 full.
  6. Bake for 20–25 minutes or until golden brown.
