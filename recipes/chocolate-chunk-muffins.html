Chocolate Chunk Muffins

Rich, bakery-style muffins with big melty chunks.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup chocolate chunks

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a muffin tin.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
  3. Mix wet ingredients in another bowl.
  4. Combine wet and dry, fold in chocolate chunks.
  5. Fill muffin cups ¾ full. Bake for 18–22 minutes.
